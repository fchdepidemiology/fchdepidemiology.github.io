District profiles ⓘ
Enrollment
The total number of students enrolled in the district for the latest week reported.

Satisfactory
Students missing less than 5% of school time.

At Risk
Students missing between 5 to 10% of school time.

Moderate
Students missing 10 to 20% of school time.

Severe
Students missing 20% or more of school time.

Chronic
Combined total of moderate and severe categories.

Change
The difference in chronically absent students compared to the previous week.
