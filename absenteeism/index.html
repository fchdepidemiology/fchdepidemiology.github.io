Fairfield County Health Department partner surveillance snapshot

Story behind the snapshot

A quick Fairfield County view to support situational awareness and district level comparison.

Highest districts at a glance

Highest and lowest chronic absenteeism rates in the current county snapshot.

District ranking chart

Districts are ranked from highest to lowest chronic absenteeism. This gives a quick countywide view of where levels are currently highest.

District profiles

Each district card shows chronic absenteeism, enrollment, change from the prior value in the state export, and the attendance mix across satisfactory, at risk, moderate, and severe categories.

Interpretation note
Absenteeism is not disease specific and may reflect illness, behavioral, transportation, family, weather, or other factors. It should be interpreted alongside other surveillance indicators when used for public health situational awareness.
